About

A multifaceted impact-driven generalist. Who delivers.

I've spent two decades doing work that is hard to put in a single box — and that is precisely the point. I've led programs in post-conflict zones, built content systems for federal agencies, managed inter-agency operations at the United Nations, and founded ventures across two continents. What drives me is a straightforward belief: that well-designed systems and well-told stories can change the conditions of people's lives. I bring that belief to every project, whether it fits neatly on a resume or not.

What sets me apart is a combination that is genuinely rare. I hold a PMP certification and a Google UX credential, but I've also directed mental health programs reaching 25,000 people and managed over a million dollars in inclusion funds across 30 countries. I'm as comfortable in a Figma prototype as I am in a multilateral negotiation. Bilingual in English and Spanish, with intermediate Mandarin, I've worked across cultures, time zones, and sectors — and consistently turned that breadth into sharper, more grounded strategy for the teams and organizations I serve.

Impact

Work that has mattered.

  • USAGov · Benefit Finder

    Making Government Benefits Findable

    As Bilingual Senior Content Strategist, I led UX content strategy for the USAGov Benefit Finder — a tool designed to help millions of Americans identify the federal programs they qualify for. My work centered on plain language, bilingual accessibility, and content structures that reduce confusion at exactly the moment people need clarity most. The tool serves one of the largest and most diverse audiences in U.S. digital government.

  • United Nations

    Reforming UN Operations Across 131 Countries

    When the United Nations undertook a sweeping reform of its global operations, I was responsible for delivering high-impact common services across all 131 UN Country Teams — spanning every major UN entity on the planet. I managed over 50 inter-agency services, coordinated across organizational boundaries that rarely cooperate, and helped translate a global mandate into functional, on-the-ground change.

  • World Bank Partnership

    Reducing Violence Through Behavioral Change

    Working alongside the World Bank and public sector partners, I directed programs that established national stress management centers serving more than 25,000 people and violence reduction initiatives that reached over 1,500 prison inmates and staff. These were not communications campaigns — they were behavioral change programs built on evidence, delivered in difficult environments, and evaluated against real outcomes.

  • Upco Create · Founder

    Building a Strategy & Creative Consultancy

    I founded Upco Create as a strategy and creative consultancy built around a single premise: that organizations achieve more when they understand their audience deeply and communicate with clarity. I've led engagements across sectors — helping clients define their positioning, build content ecosystems, and turn complex ideas into strategies that actually get executed.

  • UpImpact · Founder

    Scaling Social Impact Through Systems Thinking

    I founded UpImpact to apply the discipline of strategy and program management to the social sector — where urgency is high and resources are rarely sufficient. The work focuses on helping mission-driven organizations build the systems, metrics, and narratives they need to grow their reach and demonstrate results to funders, partners, and the communities they serve.

  • Art of Living

    Leading Product for a Global Wellness Brand

    As Product Manager for the Art of Living marketing team, I oversaw the digital product roadmap for one of the world's largest wellness and mindfulness organizations. I coordinated across content, design, and engineering to ship campaigns and platform features that served a global audience — bringing product discipline and user-centered thinking to a high-volume, mission-driven environment.
